Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This policy covers all apps published at pjecuacion.github.io, including EchoText, MarkdownForge, PicFrames, Bulk WebP, and LTD. Where an app has specific privacy considerations, they are noted below.

1. Desktop Apps — No Data Collection

All desktop applications run entirely on your machine. They do not transmit audio files, video files, images, text, or any other processing input to any server. No usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry are collected or sent.

2. License Key Validation

Pro licenses are validated by making a single network request to our license server containing only your license key. No personal data, file names, or processing results are included in this request. The validation check happens once at activation.

3. Purchases

Purchases are processed by LemonSqueezy, a third-party payment processor. When you buy a Pro license, LemonSqueezy collects your email address and payment information to fulfill your order and deliver your license key. We receive your email for order confirmation and support purposes only. We do not store payment card details. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data.

4. This Website

This site is a static GitHub Pages site. It does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics. No personal data is collected by visiting any page on this site.

5. Third-Party Services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts and Tailwind CSS from a CDN. These requests may expose your IP address to those providers under their respective privacy policies. No data is shared between those providers and us.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your email address only as long as needed for order support. To request deletion of your data, contact us at the address below.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach out via GitHub.
